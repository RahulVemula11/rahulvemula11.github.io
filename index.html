01 / Final Year Project 2025 – 2026

Anomaly-Based Video Forgery Detection

A deepfake detection system that learns from real videos only — no labelled fakes required. By framing detection as anomaly detection, it generalises to manipulation methods it has never seen. Deployed as a Flask web app with real-time verdicts.

  • Anomaly-based approach generalises to manipulation methods never seen in training
  • Convolutional Autoencoder + LSTM captures both spatial and temporal forgery cues
  • Trained on real videos only — no labelled fakes required — and runs on CPU
